About Geoscan

Geoscan Pte. Ltd. (formerly known as Geoscan Instruments Pte. Ltd.) was incorporated in Singapore in March of 1989 to meet the demand for geotechnical instrumentation and soil investigation services.

In the early years of her incorporation, the company was mostly involved in soil investigation works, such as the SBC TV World in Tuas, developments in southern islands (Pulau Seraya, Pulau Ayer Merbau, Pulau Serabok), Changi Ferry Terminal, Ngee Ann City and many infrastructure and building projects. As the demand for geotechnical works increased in 1990s, Geoscan focus its core business to include more instrumentation scope. Hence, we gave our clients a convenient and cost efficient solution to their instrumentation needs. The company has continued to grow by introducing more services, including Real Time Monitoring for Vibration, Micro-Vibration, Noise and EL-tilt meter. In order to better enhance value to our customers, Geoscan has also been involved in further studies with the Singapore scientific community to study how to better measure ground vibrations.
